# PATTERN CHAM DIEM LUAN VAN THAC SI - IUH
## Truong Dai Hoc Cong Nghiep TP. Ho Chi Minh

**Tong diem: 100 diem | Thang diem 10 (chia 10)**

---

## I. HINH THUC VA CAU TRUC LUAN VAN (15 diem)

### 1.1 Trang bia va phu bia (2 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Day du thong tin: Bo Cong Thuong, ten truong, logo IUH, ten tac gia, ten de tai, nganh, ma nganh, nam | 1 |
| Dinh dang dung quy dinh (font, co chu, canh le) | 1 |

### 1.2 Cac trang bat buoc (3 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Co trang Hoi dong cham (ghi ro 5 thanh vien: Chu tich, 2 Phan bien, Uy vien, Thu ky) | 0.5 |
| Co trang Nhiem vu luan van thac si (ten de tai, nhiem vu, noi dung, ngay giao/hoan thanh, nguoi huong dan) | 0.5 |
| Co Loi cam on | 0.5 |
| Co Loi cam doan | 0.5 |
| Co Tom tat luan van (tieng Viet) | 0.5 |
| Co Abstract (tieng Anh) | 0.5 |

### 1.3 Danh muc (3 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Muc luc day du, dung so trang | 1 |
| Danh muc hinh anh (co so thu tu, ten, so trang) | 0.5 |
| Danh muc bang bieu (co so thu tu, ten, so trang) | 0.5 |
| Danh muc tu viet tat (ky hieu, chu viet day du, y nghia) | 0.5 |
| Danh muc cong trinh da cong bo cua hoc vien | 0.5 |

### 1.4 Trinh bay (4 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Font chu, co chu, giãn dong dung quy dinh | 1 |
| Canh le trang dung chuan | 0.5 |
| Danh so trang lien tuc, dung quy cach | 0.5 |
| Hinh anh, bang bieu ro rang, co chu thich day du | 1 |
| Cong thuc toan hoc trinh bay dung chuan, co danh so | 1 |

### 1.5 Ly lich trich ngang (1 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Co day du thong tin ca nhan, qua trinh dao tao, cong tac | 1 |

### 1.6 So trang (2 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Luan van dat so trang toi thieu (>= 60 trang noi dung chinh) | 1 |
| Can doi giua cac chuong, khong chuong nao qua ngan hoac qua dai | 1 |

## II. MO DAU (15 diem)

### 2.1 Dat van de (3 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Trinh bay ro tinh cap thiet cua de tai | 1 |
| Lien he thuc tien, dan chung so lieu/tham khao cu the | 1 |
| Lap luan logic, mach lac dan den van de nghien cuu | 1 |

### 2.2 Muc tieu nghien cuu (3 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Muc tieu tong quat ro rang, cu the | 1 |
| Cac muc tieu cu the (objectives) ro, do luong duoc | 1 |
| Muc tieu phu hop voi ten de tai | 1 |

### 2.3 Doi tuong va pham vi nghien cuu (2 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Xac dinh ro doi tuong nghien cuu | 1 |
| Pham vi nghien cuu cu the (khong gian, thoi gian, noi dung) | 1 |

### 2.4 Phuong phap nghien cuu (3 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Mo ta phuong phap tong luan (literature review) | 1 |
| Mo ta phuong phap thuc nghiem/mo phong cu the | 1 |
| Moi truong thuc nghiem (phan cung, phan mem) duoc neu ro | 1 |

### 2.5 Y nghia thuc tien (2 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Neu ro dong gop khoa hoc | 1 |
| Neu ro gia tri ung dung thuc te | 1 |

### 2.6 Pham vi, gioi han de tai va cau truc luan van (2 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Neu ro gioi han cua de tai | 1 |
| Mo ta cau truc luan van (liet ke cac chuong va noi dung chinh) | 1 |

---

## III. CO SO LY THUYET / TONG QUAN (15 diem)

### 3.1 Tong quan tinh hinh nghien cuu (5 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Tham khao day du cac cong trinh lien quan trong va ngoai nuoc | 2 |
| Phan tich uu/nhuoc diem cua cac nghien cuu truoc | 1.5 |
| Chi ra khoang trong (research gap) ma de tai se giai quyet | 1.5 |

### 3.2 Co so ly thuyet (5 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Trinh bay dung, day du cac khai niem nen tang | 2 |
| Cong thuc toan hoc chinh xac, co giai thich | 1.5 |
| Ly thuyet phu hop voi de tai, khong lan man | 1.5 |

### 3.3 Cong cu/Phan mem (2 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Gioi thieu phan mem/cong cu su dung (Eviews, Python, TensorFlow, v.v.) | 1 |
| Giai thich ly do chon cong cu nay | 1 |

### 3.4 Chat luong tham khao (3 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Tai lieu tham khao tu tap chi uy tin (ISI/Scopus/IEEE/ACM) | 1 |
| So luong tai lieu tham khao phu hop (>= 20 tai lieu) | 1 |
| Tai lieu moi, cap nhat (trong vong 5 nam gan nhat chiem >= 50%) | 1 |

---

## IV. PHUONG PHAP / MO HINH DE XUAT (20 diem)

### 4.1 Mo hinh de xuat (8 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Mo ta kien truc mo hinh/he thong ro rang | 2 |
| Co so do kien truc tong the (architecture diagram) | 2 |
| Giai thich chi tiet tung thanh phan/module | 2 |
| Tinh moi/sang tao so voi cac nghien cuu truoc | 2 |

### 4.2 Quy trinh thuc hien (6 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Mo ta quy trinh thu thap du lieu ro rang | 1.5 |
| Tien xu ly du lieu (data preprocessing) day du | 1.5 |
| Trich xuat dac trung (feature engineering) hop ly | 1.5 |
| Tham so mo hinh duoc giai thich | 1.5 |

### 4.3 Phuong phap danh gia (6 diem)
| Tieu chi | Diem |
|----------|------|
| Chon do do danh gia phu hop (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1, AUC, MSE, MAE, v.v.) | 2 |
| Giai thich ly do chon tung do do | 2 |
| Co phuong phap so sanh voi baseline/mo hinh khac | 2 |

## GHI CHU DANH GIA

### Cac yeu to CONG diem:
- Co bai bao khoa hoc da cong bo lien quan den de tai (tap chi/hoi thao trong nuoc hoac quoc te)
- Su dung du lieu thuc te tu doanh nghiep/to chuc
- Mo hinh/phuong phap co tinh moi, sang tao cao
- Co ung dung thuc te da trien khai hoac prototype

### Cac yeu to TRU diem:
- Sao chep (plagiarism) > 20%
- Thieu co so ly thuyet, chi trinh bay ung dung don thuan
- Ket qua thuc nghiem khong the tai lap (not reproducible)
- Khong co so sanh voi cac phuong phap khac
- Loi chinh ta, ngu phap nhieu
